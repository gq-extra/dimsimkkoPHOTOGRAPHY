Hello, I'm Dimitri. I'm a passionate photographer who loves to capture the beauty of nature, people, and architecture. I've been in this field for over a year and have experience in various genres of photography. My goal is to create images that tell stories and evoke emotions. I believe that every picture has a unique story to tell, and it's my job to bring that story to life.

About My Camera

I use Nikon D5100 for most of my shoots. It's a powerful camera that allows me to capture high-quality images with ease. It has a 16.2-megapixel DX-format CMOS sensor, 11-point autofocus system, and a 3-inch LCD screen. I also want to have a collection of lenses that I will use for different types of photography.

Motivation

Photography is not just a hobby or a profession for me. It's a way of life. Every time I pick up my camera, I feel alive. It's like I'm seeing the world in a different way. Photography has taught me to appreciate the beauty of the world, and it has helped me connect with people from all walks of life. Through my images, I hope to inspire others to see the world through a different lens and appreciate the beauty that surrounds us.
